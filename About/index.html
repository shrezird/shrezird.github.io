Our Story

Shrezird Games is a small independent game studio ran by two very close friends, shrezird and FoxSenpaii. FoxSenpaii is the talented visual artist that brings all the life into the simple things and shrezird just makes things work... sometimes. The studio first launched under the name of Xebaro Studios in late 2023 and was changed to Shrezird Games in early 2024. The studio has since changed drastically and many game ideas have come and gone. But through it all we’re proud to still be working with two other great contributors, sk3l3ton._ for music and Hammond for his immense talent as a voice actor. Currently we are mostly working with the Roblox Studio engine, but we’re starting to use a fantastic open source engine known as Godot as well. At this time we have no published games from either engine, however we’re planning a few games with no set release date. Anyways enough about us, be sure and stay tuned by checking out our other social media accounts below!
