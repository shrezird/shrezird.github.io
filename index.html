About Us

Shrezird Games is an independent game development studio based in the United States, focusing on creating the next cool 2D game. We do almost every bit of our development from scratch and prefer to use GameMaker as our primary engine of choice. At this time we are only working with 2D but we have plans to move to 3D eventually. If you would like to see our latest projects and sneak peaks then check out our socials linked at the top right of the page! Have a good one <3!
